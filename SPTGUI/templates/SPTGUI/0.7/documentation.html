Spot-On documentation

The official Spot-On documentation.

Spot-On allows you to analyze single particle tracking datasets. Spot-On fits a realistic kinetic model to the distribution of jump lengths and provides estimates of the fraction bound (\(F\)) and diffusion coefficients (\(D\)) for either a two state (bound-free) or a three state (bound-free1-free2) model.

Spot-On is a libre/open-source software and exists both as a web-application and a command-line version.
